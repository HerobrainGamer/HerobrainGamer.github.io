The Restart Service Script

#restart-service.ps1
#Created : May 27th 2020
#Updated : May 27th 2020
#By : William

$service = Read-Host "Name of the service you want to restart"

Restart-Service -Name $service -Verbose

What It Does

Well this script restart whatever service you provide to it, when it asks you for the
"Name of the service you want to restart" and passes that server into the Restart-Service command.
