The Restart Spooler Script

#Restart-Spooler.ps1
#Date : May 25, 2020
#Updated : May 27th 2020
#By : William

#Synopsis: This script restarts print spooler for troubleshooting.

Restart-Service -Name Spooler -Verbose
Get-Service -Name Spooler

What It Does

Well this script restart the spooler service witch is the service
that handles sending files to printers and telling it to print the file.
