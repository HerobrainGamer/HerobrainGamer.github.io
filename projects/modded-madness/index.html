Beta 0.17 Is Here :)

The modpack that is the all-around giant kitchen sink modpack with so many mods that you won't know what to do with them all but we've got everything from tech, magic, and random stuff. The one thing that we will recommend is to try and defeat all the bosses and build a great base while doing so and maybe automate everything.
